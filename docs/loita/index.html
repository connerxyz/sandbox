Lost out in the abyss

A commission from the late 2000s to create images for the album Lost in the Abyss, music composed and performed by the band Orca.

Elements

The album's recordings were largely live studio performances featuring extended improvisational sections with psychedelic elements. These themes were incorporated into the visuals.

Concept

The more conceptual part of the work forms a loose pattern when combined with copies of itself. One image evokes individual experience and mortality, while the pattern left to be discovered evokes interconnections across experience with no real beginning or end.

Print

Posters, album covers, and other materials incorporating the image.
