Codex

Codex is an app for building and exploring personal knowledge graphs using digital artifacts, like text, images, and video. What its inventor, Iian Neil, calls a knowledge worker's operating system. Here's how we went about discovering a brand and visual identity for Codex.

Mood

A moodboard helps identify visual elements and discover a general direction for the brand identity. Codex delivers aha moments to users by connecting the new and old in surprising ways for example â€“ and we leaned into anachronism as a defining characteristic of Codex's identity.

Color

A variety of potential color palettes were sampled from the moodboard. Gold was then selected as a primary brand color to serve a dual purpose. It built on the idea of Codex being anachronistic (gold is both old and timeless), also gold, as a neutral color, left room for the many other secondary and tertiary colors Codex needed to render its user-interface.
