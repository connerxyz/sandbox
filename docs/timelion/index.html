timelion

Complex Complex is a jazz metal trio, part of a raucous, experimental American midwest/east-coast music scene that began in the late 2000's. The band and I toured through Chicago, Pittsburgh, Philadelphia, and New York together many times...

I was commissioned sometime around 2008 to produce images for their concept album, Timelion.
